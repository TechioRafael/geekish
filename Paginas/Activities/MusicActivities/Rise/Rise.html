Rise

Rise is one of the theme songs of Worldwide League of Legends in 2018. It is a joint production between The Glitch Mob, Mako and The World alive and the lyrics was written by Riot Games feat Alex Seaver, from Mako.

The music came with a video and it made an honor to Pro players who marked the history of the game, for example, the Korean player called Faker, from SKT, who is considered the best player of all times and, in the year of 2018, was out of the competition.

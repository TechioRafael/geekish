About IFPR

IFPR is a federal institution that offers technical courses, such as computing. The purpose of the school is to offer high-qualit learning with professionals in courses that it makes.

This website was born from an integrated project that involves tree subjects - English Language, Web Development and Computer's Network.

We'd like to appreciate the work of our teachers, in special the English Language Teacher, Patricia Ramos, and the Web Development Teacher, Nelson. Without these teachers, this website wouldn’t exist. Thank You! We love you and your patience with us!

Objective

The primary objective of this project is make a professional site that can help people to learn and practice English in a different way and, can evolve the students in that three subjects whose make part of this project.
